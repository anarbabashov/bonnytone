We would like to offer **free access to our radio stream API** for integration into your platform. Our stream is available in multiple formats to ensure maximum compatibility:

- **MP3/AAC direct stream** via Icecast for traditional directory integration

All stream endpoints and API documentation can be provided upon request. Integration is straightforward and we are happy to support your technical team through the process.

### Our Vision & Growth Plans

We are music lovers at heart, and our mission is to bring quality dance music to everyone, everywhere. BonnyTone Radio is just the beginning — we have ambitious plans to expand our network with **two additional stations** in the near future:

1. **BonnyTone Lounge** — downtempo, ambient, chillout, and lounge music for relaxed listening
2. **BonnyTone Techno** — techno, minimal, and harder-edged electronic sounds

As our network grows, your platform would automatically benefit from additional content and genre diversity at no extra cost.

### Our Approach to Monetization

BonnyTone Radio is currently a **completely free product** — no paywalls, no sales funnels, no subscriptions. Our focus right now is on building the best possible listening experience and growing our community of dance music lovers.

As the project grows, we plan to introduce **optional premium features** for a small monthly fee — such as exclusive DJ sets, lossless audio quality, and personalized playlists. The core radio experience will always remain free.

When that time comes, **we would be happy to offer a commission to our early partners** who believed in us and helped us reach our audience. We value loyalty, and the platforms that support us from the start will be the first to benefit as BonnyTone grows.

### Tier 1 — Submit First

| Platform | How to Submit | Notes |
|----------|--------------|-------|
| **TuneIn** | [broadcasters.tunein.com/stations/add](https://broadcasters.tunein.com/stations/add) | Logo must be 1200x1200px min. AzuraCast has native TuneIn webhook for Now Playing metadata. Email broadcaster-support@tunein.com for API credentials. |
| **Radio Garden** | [submit.radio.garden](https://submit.radio.garden/) | Provide MP3/AAC stream (HLS NOT accepted). Stream must be 24/7. Website must show contact info. |
| **RadioBrowser** | [radio-browser.info/add](https://www.radio-browser.info/add) | Near-instant listing. Open-source DB powering many apps. Provide direct stream URL + favicon URL. |
| **Streema / Simple Radio** | [streema.com/request](https://streema.com/request) | Also powers Simple Radio mobile app (millions of users). Square logo, under 1MB. |
